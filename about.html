about me

I am a passionate swiss graphic designer with a keen interest in typography and the power of individual graphic characters in communication. My Bachelor’s thesis was an exploration of the synaesthetic properties of letters, which helped me develop a unique and personal approach to representing letters through pictures and animations.

I am always eager to take on new challenges and push my boundaries, and I strive to stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and techniques. So, if you are looking for a dedicated and talented graphic designer to bring your vision to life, please don’t hesitate to get in touch!
